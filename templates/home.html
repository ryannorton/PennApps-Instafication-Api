What is InstantTM?

The Instant Button is a chrome extension that appears alongside your favorite online retailers. InstantTM searches for the product you want in your local stores and makes a call to PostMates to deliver the item to you within an hour.

In short, Instant combines the convenience of online shopping with the speed of local delivery.
